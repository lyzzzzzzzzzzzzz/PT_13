Michele Magdato

How was the school year 2021 - 2022 for me? I can best describe it as a bicycle ride.
I realized that if I want to get things done, I should keep the balance going.
I learned that playing too much video game can lead me to a failing grade,
and if grinding and getting my school works done with little rest or entertainment can
lead me to sleep deprivation and exhaustion. Because of the online learning,
I barely learned lessons that were taught to us by our teachers. But instead,
I learned life lessons that are worth keeping in mind. There is a time for everything,
which means we shouldn’t be doing the things we aren’t supposed to at the wrong moment.
We need balance in our life. Too much of something can be exhausting, we’ll feel worn out, and tired.
We also need rest from our school works, but not too much. We need to work on our school outputs,
but not too much. I hope the ones who read this would be struck to heart.
