THE BATTLES

This year's Battles did not solely rely on bands but adapted to tiktok and lipsynching.
Each grade level were to enter a film with one OPM, a Pop, and a K-Pop song (All should
vary). Our batch took "Peklat Cream" by Bita and the Botflies, "Maniac" by Conan Gray,
and "Alcohol Free" by TWICE as their main songs and groups. Bringing the performances to
its outmost potential awarded us with third place in rankings and champion by heart .
